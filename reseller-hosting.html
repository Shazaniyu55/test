What is NVMe SSD?

With up to 6 x better performance compared to classic SSD disks, NVMe drives are the new benchmark for fast storage. Like traditional SSDs, NVMe (Non-Volatile Memory Express) disks are based on NAND Flash memory. By sitting directly on top of the PCI Express bus, previously used by other devices like graphics cards, NVMe disks are not affected by the bottlenecks of the SATA interface.cheapest web hosting india

What is the difference between KVM Linux VPS and Standard VPS?

KVM Linux VPS is more like a dedicated server within another physical dedicated server. Standard VPS (OpenVZ) shares resources with every other client on the host node and is considered a container-based virtualization.
